Brand Guidelines

Primary Actions
Use the teal-to-orange gradient for primary CTAs. This creates visual hierarchy and draws attention to conversion points.
Color Balance
60% neutral (whites/grays), 30% primary teal, 10% accent orange. This creates a professional yet festive appearance.
Typography Hierarchy
Maintain clear visual hierarchy with consistent font weights. Headlines bold (600-700), body regular (400), CTAs semi-bold (600).
Seasonal Flexibility
Colors work year-round but emphasize warm tones (orange/red) during holiday season for festive feeling.
Accessibility
Ensure WCAG AA compliance. Teal (#147878) on white provides 4.5:1 contrast ratio. Use darker shades for body text.
White Space
Generous padding creates premium feel. Use minimum 16px padding on mobile, 24px on desktop for breathing room.
